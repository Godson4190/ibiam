About Us

The company offers long term and short term investment opportunities, BlueGate Financial Service was established in 2002 and it initially began trading stocks, shares and bonds, it got involved in forex trading in 2008 and was among the pioneer traders of Crypto-currencies in the advent of Bitcoin in 2010.
